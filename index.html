02 — The Product

Three components.
One seamless system.

Mouth Guard Bone conduction microphone captures speech before it leaves the mouth. Suppresses outward sound. Enables silent input.
Neural Case On-device GPU (Jetson Orin Nano). Whisper + Llama 7B + XTTS v2. No cloud. No data transfer. ~500ms latency.
Micro Earbuds Incoming speech translated in parallel, delivered silently. No visible hardware. No shared device required.
Voice Clone Translation delivered in your authentic voice. Listener hears you — not a robotic proxy. Trained on-device, no audio saved.
Ghost Mode EMG subvocal recognition. Speak without producing sound. Target: Phase 2, months 24–32. Zero audio signature.
